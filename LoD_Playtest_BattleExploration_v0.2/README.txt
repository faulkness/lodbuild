Legend of Dragoon Remake – Playable Battle & Exploration Test (v0.2)

CONTROLS:
- Left Stick / D-Pad: Move
- X: Confirm
- Triangle: Dragoon (Birdman only)

HOW TO PLAY:
1. Load project in Godot 4
2. Open 'scenes/Main.tscn'
3. Walk to trigger zone to start battle
4. Use Dart’s attack or Birdman’s Dragoon form

Enjoy!
